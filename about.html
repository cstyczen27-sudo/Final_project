Our Mission

Adrian College Event Finder is a website that lets Adrian College Event Hosts display their events to students of Adrian College, so they can see what fun events are coming up and save the events that they are interested in taking part in.

Welcome to Adrian Campus Events, the one-stop shop for all of your Campus events needs. When I first arrived at Adrian College, I met so many great and amazing people that I wanted to hang out with every day. One small problem was that, even though I met people, I never socialized outside my circle, so I never really knew when or where anything was happening. My solution? Using my ability to code and build a website, of course.

My hope is that in the future, people will use this app to solve the issue I had in college: wanting to plan events with friends, whether it's a sporting event or just something fun the school is throwing.

And I hope that, if this idea ever reaches someone on the board of Adrian College, we can work together to make it a reality and turn it into an official Adrian College Event Finder app, making planning and having fun with friends easier at Adrian College.
